About Me

  • Hi, I'm Caden!

    I'm a student at EWU and I'm majoring in Design with a minor in business. I currently work for EWU Theatre as their publicity and social media manager.

  • Hobbies

    I've recently found a love for photography and I've been cosplaying since high school. I always learn something new with each new cosplay, like sewing or leather painting. I also love to draw and paint with gouache.

  • Digital Skills

    I love using Adobe Lighroom Classic and Photoshop to edit my photos. I also use Illustrator and InDesign for graphic design work. I've used Premiere Pro for a few projects too.

Past School Work

  • Poster Redesign

    In my Visual Thinking class we had to redesign a well known movie poster. I chose the Wizard of Oz becuse it work well with the collage and limited color requirements of the poster.

  • Logo Design

    In VCD 1 We had to Design logos for our partner or "client." His company was a hotsauce company. He knew the name would be "Satan's Oasis" but we had to do a lot of research and sketching to find the visual elements we liked. We decided to go with a warm summery vibe. We liked the idea of going satanic but we figured it could scare costumers so we focused on the fire and the oasis part.

  • Photography

    This is the first image I captued and edited in my Into to Photography class. I absolutely fell in love with photography and it quickly became my favorite class. It's a skill I'll use forever. I use it for my job, to document my cosplays and just for fun! I'm excited to grow as a photographer!

Job History

  • EWU Theatre

    Currently, I work at EWU Theatre as their PR and Social Media Manager. I design the posters, program and any other graphic they might need. I take photos of the cast and document the process of putting a show together. I also run all of their socials including Facebook, Instagram and Tiktok.

  • North Bend Theatre

    October 2019-August 2021 I worked as Lobby Crew at this historic one room theatre. I cleaned, served concessions, and even ran the projector. I still go back to work there over the summers. I love this theatre and the people who run it. This theatre brings the community together.

  • Mount Si High School Drama

    Since freshman year I was apart of the MSHS Drama club. This is where I found my love for poster design. I designed their posters, programs, t-shirts and ran their social media. I also became Vice President of the club my Senior year. My Junior and Senior year were overshadowed by the covid-19 pandemic but we stayed connected during that dark time.
